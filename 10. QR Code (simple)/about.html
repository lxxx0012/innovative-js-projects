About BrickMMO

BrickMMO was created by a community of LEGO enthusiasts across the communications with the goal of providing a central hub for learning about LEGO building and discovering exciting local and global events.

Our mission is to inspire creativity, foster learning through play, and connect LEGO builders of all ages and skills levels.

Our Team

We are small team of dedicated LEGO fans who volunteer our time to maintain and grow this platform. We believe in the power of LEGO to educate, entertain, and bring people together.

Join Our Community

Whether you're a seasoned Master Builder or just starting your LEGO journey, we welcome you to BrickMMo. Explore our learning resources, find upcoming events, and connect with fellow builders!
